About This Map

This is a visualization songs that I associate with specific places in my memory, plotted on an interactive, 3-D globe. Feel free to zoom and pan around the globe to explore!

The songs are color-coded according to sentiment analysis of their lyrics, with yellow representing positive sentiment and blue representing negative sentiment.
